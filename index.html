Our Sweet Journey

1995 — A Kemptville Beginning

Founded by the Kiwi family with classic recipes and a love for community, starting as a small shop in downtown Kemptville.

2005 — Growing the Dream

Expanded our space and selection, becoming a local favourite for pick-and-mix candy by the pound.

2015 — Online & Canada-Wide

Launched our e-commerce shop so Canadians from coast to coast could enjoy Kiwi’s confections.

2025 — Sustainable & Local

Committed to eco-friendly packaging and supporting Ontario suppliers while keeping our handcrafted quality.

Our Mission

To make joyful, memorable confectionery — the kind you share — while supporting our Kemptville community and operating responsibly.

Our Values

  • Craftsmanship in every batch
  • Friendly, Canadian customer care
  • Environmental responsibility
  • Community-minded and local first
